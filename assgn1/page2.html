Favorite Movies

Bodies Bodies Bodies

Bodies Bodies Bodies is a dark comedy horror film where a group of friends' hurricane party turns deadly during a game of "Bodies Bodies Bodies," leading to real murder and chaos. The film blends satire with suspense, exploring themes of trust and betrayal amidst a night of escalating paranoia.

Midsommar

Midsommar is a 2019 horror film directed by Ari Aster, centered on a couple who travel to a remote Swedish village for a midsummer festival that quickly devolves into a disturbing and sinister experience. The film combines psychological horror with striking daylight scenes, exploring themes of grief, relationships, and cultural disintegration.
